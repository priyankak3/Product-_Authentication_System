Manufacturer

  • Add new products to your inventory easily with this option. Simply fill out the required details, including product name, description, price, and image, and submit the form. Your new product will be added to your inventory and made available for sale to consumers..


    Add Product

  • Register new sellers to your platform using this option. Collect important information from sellers, such as their contact details, business information, and product offerings, to create their seller profiles. This will enable sellers to list their products and start selling to consumers through your platform.


    Add Seller

  • Provide sellers with the option to purchase products directly from you with this feature. Sellers can browse through your product catalog, select the items they wish to purchase, and complete the transaction online. This streamlines the process for sellers and allows them to quickly restock their inventory with high-quality products.


    Sell Product To Seller

  • Access detailed information about registered sellers using this option. Simply enter the seller's unique ID or search by their name to retrieve their profile. You can view important details about the seller, including their contact information, business details, and product offerings.
